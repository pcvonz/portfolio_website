Secutor

Secutor is an app that is in ongoing development.

The brief

Secutor was inspired by the Pomodoro Technique. The twist is that it triggers an event whenever a pomodoro is completed. I decided to build upon my web knowledge and try out ElectronJS. As of now, it connects to the Habitica API and ticks off an item that Habitica keeps track of.

Research

Initial design mockups were created and shared with some community members. Feedback was implemented into the design.

Technology Used

  • ElectronJS
  • Gulp
  • RiotJS
