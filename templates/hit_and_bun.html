Hit & Bun

The brief

As a school project I was tasked with designing a website for Hit and bun, a fictional food truck based in Denver Colorado. With the branding given to me, I designed a single page website that took into account a users potential needs.

Research

Competitive Analysis

Most food trucks in the Denver area push the schedule to the top of the page followed by the menu. The catering section usually prompts the user to send an email or submit a form.

Social media is important to inform users of the current location of the truck and to establish rapport with customers. Most websites put their social media at the bottom of the page, but I wanted to integrate it into the header so users immediately see the feed.

Scenarios

The user scenarios I identified were:

  • Finding the location of the food truck.
  • Viewing the menu.
  • Booking the truck to cater an event.

Implementation

The mobile website is laid out like a standard phone application. The menu is done completely in CSS.

The desktop website features an embedded and customized twitter feed at the top of the page
