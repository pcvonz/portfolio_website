Faxian

The brief

As a school project, I developed a branding system and website for a fictional company that dealt with Sustainability Consulting.

Research

Competitors

The landscape of design in sustainability consulting is pretty barren. Most companies use a leaf motif with a wordmark. I wanted Faxian to stand out but still utilize what works. Keep the elements that identify the company as environmentally conscious, but emphasize their business savvy.

Brand direction

  • Bold serious font
  • Illustrative elements

Implementation

I created the website using Susy, a small grid system written for SASS. I also implemented a jquery plugin called fullpage.js in order to highlight the different services Faxian provides. On desktop the user gets a rather neat slideshow, on mobile the plugin is disabled so they read through it without unexpected behavior.
